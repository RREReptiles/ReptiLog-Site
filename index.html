Offline-First Architecture

Your data is always available.
No signal? No problem.

Reptile rooms, basements, and remote facilities often have poor or zero reception. ReptiLog stores everything locally on your device first, so you can log feedings, record weights, and update animal records without ever touching a cell tower.

When connectivity returns, our cloud sync engine — powered by Supabase — quietly reconciles your data in the background. No conflicts, no data loss, no interruptions.

  • SQLite local database — instant reads & writes
  • Supabase cloud sync — real-time when connected
  • Conflict-free data reconciliation
  • Your data — never sold, never shared
